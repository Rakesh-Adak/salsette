The Start of Salsette

Salsette Vodka, a homegrown premium spirit from Mumbai, captures the very essence of the city that makes it sublime in every mix and every occasion. A world-class vodka, made for anyone looking to have a good time, responsibly.

Get a taste of the spirit of Salsette, today.

Refining with nature

Five times filtered through coconut shell activated carbon for an extra-smooth blend.

Distilling the finest spirits

Seven times distilled and made using ultra-purified demineralized water to achieve the highest level of purity.

From the land, for the land

Crafted using high-quality rice from Western Maharashtra for an impeccable flavour profile.

Untether the layers of Salsette with every mix

Whether you prefer your vodka neat or mixed in your own special way,
have a taste of one of the many possibilities that #StartWithSalsette

Cosmopolitan Cocktail

Steps:

  1. Mix Salsette vodka, lime juice, triple sec, and cranberry juice in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Add ice, cover and shake until chilled. Strain into a chilled cocktail glass.
  3. Garnish with a lime wedge.
  4. You're ready with your classic take on Bombay cosmopolitan.

Sea Breeze

Steps:

  1. Add the Salsette Vodka, cranberry juice and grapefruit juice into a highball glass with ice.
  2. Stir gently until the outside of the glass feels cold.
  3. Garnish with a lime wheel.
  4. You're ready to sip the Sea Breeze.

Sex on the Beach

Steps:

  1. Mix Salsette Vodka, peach schnapps, orange juice and cranberry juice in a cocktail shaker.
  2. Top it up with ice and shake, Strain into a highball glass over fresh ice.
  3. Garnish with a cocktail umbrella and an orange wedge to serve.
  4. The iconic Sex On The Beach cocktail is ready to serve.

Screwdriver

Steps:

  1. Fill a highball glass with ice.
  2. Add Salsette vodka and orange juice and stir to mix.
  3. Garnish it with an orange slice.
  4. Loosen up memories last with a classic Screwdriver cocktail.

Salsette Party Punch

Steps:

  1. Mix Salsette vodka, Hawaiian Punch, pineapple juice, Ginger Ale and orange juice together in a pitcher.
  2. Add 1 sliced orange and 1 sliced lemon, stir to combine.
  3. Add crushed ice to glasses and top with a punch.
  4. If making ahead, chill the pitcher of punch until serving.
